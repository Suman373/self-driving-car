Modes

  • Simulation Mode: Shows a single car for testing or demonstration.
  • Training Mode: Uses multiple cars for parallel AI training.

Controls

  • Toggle training (⚙️): Switches between Simulation (1 car) and Training (many cars).
  • Toggle car sensor (🔦): Toggles the visibility of the sensor rays on the active car.
  • Toggle control type (🧠): Switches the active car between AI control and manual control (using keyboard arrow keys).
  • Save car brain (🗃️): Saves the neural network of the best-performing AI car to your browser's storage.
  • Discard car brain (🗑️): Deletes the saved neural network.
  • Restart (🔃): Resets the active car, useful for getting back on the road after a crash.
